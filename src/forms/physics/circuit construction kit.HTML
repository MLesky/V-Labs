Building a Simple Circuit

Here are the steps to perform the Experiment on Building a Simple Circuit:

    Aim:o build a simple circuit using Circuit Construction Kit and observe how it works

    Materials:Computer with internet access, Circuit Construction Kit simulation software (available online)

    Procedures

  1. Open the Circuit Construction Kit simulation software on your computer.
  2. Click on the "DC" power source icon in the toolbar on the left side of the screen and drag it onto the workspace.
  3. Click on the "wire" icon in the toolbar and connect one end of the wire to the positive (+) terminal of the power source and the other end to a light bulb.
  4. Connect another wire from the negative (-) terminal of the power source to the other end of the light bulb.
  5. Click on the "run" button in the toolbar to turn on the circuit and observe how the light bulb lights up.

    6. Observation

    When the circuit is turned on, the light bulb should light up, indicating that the circuit is complete and electricity is flowing through it.
